## 🌌 The Core Philosophy

Traditional voting systems are 1-dimensional (Yes/No). They ignore the "tension" and "balance" required for a sustainable society. Project Lattice redefines social states as **complex vectors** within a 4-axis tetrahedron model.

By leveraging **Complex-Valued Logic**, we allow for:

* **Real axis ():** Tangible impacts (Economic Growth vs. Social Justice).
* **Imaginary axis ():** Intangible values (Technological Disruption vs. Cultural Preservation).

We believe that governance is not about winning a majority; it is about **minimizing the distortion of the social crystal.**
